Turing Scholars Honors Program

The Turing Scholars Program is an honors program for outstanding computer science (CS) undergraduates. Housed in the nation's 8th ranked CS department, the program's mission is to provide a CS education that is second to none.

» Why Turing?

Turing combines the advantages of a small college—small class sizes, close interaction with faculty, and honors housing—with the advantages of a large research university, including a large variety of undergraduate and graduate courses, and ample opportunities for interdisciplinary studies.

» Academics

The program provides an intense and challenging curriculum that culminates with the defense of an undergraduate research thesis.

» Student Life

The Turing Scholars Program supports a strong social life. The small class size fosters a tight-knit community within each class, and weekly social events help facilitate interactions between incoming students and older Turing Scholars.

» Contact Us

Have any questions? Don't hesitate to reach out to learn more about our program.
